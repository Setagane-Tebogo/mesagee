💌 A Message For Fifi

Hey Fifi, hope you’re okay.

I’ve been thinking for some time before sending this message because I didn’t want to disturb your peace. I just wanted us to be okay. I’m not saying you’re not okay, but from my side I wanted to make sure there’s no anger, tension, or pain left between us.

I want to say I’m sorry. I acknowledge my mistakes and the things I did that hurt you. If possible, please find it in your heart to forgive me.

Thank you for everything you did for me. Thank you for keeping my secrets safe. Thank you for understanding parts of me I didn’t show everyone. I’ll always appreciate that.

The moments we spent together, the laughs, the conversations, and the memories meant a lot to me.

I understand maybe I became too much sometimes. If I made you feel tired or unhappy, I’m sorry.

I know you moved on and I genuinely hope you’re happy. I hope the person beside you treats you with kindness and gives you peace.

As for me, I don’t know what the future holds, but I believe maybe one day in the future I may need you..not in a relationship way, but maybe as someone I can speak to or ask for help. That’s why I don’t want us to leave things with anger or tension between us because of the breakup. I just wanted to make sure we’re okay.

I’m not asking for anything. I just wanted peace between us.

Have a blessed night, Fifi. May God continue to bless and protect you always ❤️
